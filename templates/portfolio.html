Portfolio Analysis

Upload your Fidelity positions CSV to get sector allocation, concentration risk analysis, diversification gaps, and opportunity suggestions.

Disclaimer: Your CSV is processed in-memory and never stored. Analysis uses Yahoo Finance data. Sector/industry classifications and analyst targets are not guarantees. Always do your own research before investing.

Your data stays in your browser session and is not stored. Account numbers are stripped before upload.

How to download your positions CSV from Fidelity
  1. Log in to Fidelity.com and go to Accounts & Trade → Portfolio.
  2. Make sure you're viewing the Positions tab (this is the default view).
  3. If you have multiple accounts, select the specific account you want to analyze, or choose All Accounts.
  4. Click the download icon (small arrow pointing down) near the top-right of the positions table.
  5. Select Download to save the CSV file to your computer.
  6. Upload the downloaded .csv file using the form above.

Privacy note: The CSV from Fidelity includes your account number. Our tool automatically strips account numbers and other sensitive fields in your browser before anything is sent to the server. Only ticker symbols, share counts, and dollar values are transmitted for analysis.

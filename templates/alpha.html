How Mosaic Alpha™ works

Mosaic Alpha is a weighted composite of 13 factor signals, each scored 0–100:

Fundamentals

  • Value (10%) — P/E, P/B, PEG, EV/EBITDA vs. peers
  • Quality (10%) — ROE, margins, balance sheet, FCF
  • Growth (8%) — Revenue & earnings growth, PEG
  • Earnings Surprise (8%) — Beat rate and surprise magnitude

Smart Money

  • Insider Activity (12%) — Insider buy/sell ratio
  • Institutional (7%) — Ownership % and holder changes
  • Buyback (7%) — Share count changes over 1–3 years

Market Signals

  • Momentum (8%) — 3/6/12-month price performance
  • Technical (6%) — RSI, MACD crossovers, SMA trends
  • Analyst (5%) — Consensus rating and target upside
  • Analyst Trend (5%) — Recent upgrade/downgrade momentum

Environment

  • Industry Cycle (7%) — Sector ETF cycle phase
  • Macro (7%) — VIX regime and yield curve shape

Every stock analyzed is stored in a local SQLite database, building a historical record that enables industry-relative comparisons and forward-return analysis over time.
